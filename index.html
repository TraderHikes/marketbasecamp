03 · Professional Trading Journal

Every entry, exit, and pyramid — mapped automatically.

Stop reconstructing your trades from memory at month-end. Market Basecamp logs every action — entries, partial exits, pyramids — and turns it into a full journal with realised P&L, win rate, and profit factor.

◆
Trade journey timeline — initial entry, every add and partial exit, and final exit with running P&L for each step.
◆
Annotated trade cards — write your thesis ("strong recovery after post-result sell-off") right alongside the chart.
◆
Portfolio-level stats — win rate, profit factor, average return, and max profit/loss across your full trading history.
